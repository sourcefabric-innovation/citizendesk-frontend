  2. Time zone

All time data showed in the application are converted to your time zone. The time zone cannot be customised by this application, being it a setting of the operative system.

This means that you have to configure the time zone either in Android or in iOS, Windows, Linux, or the system installed on your machine.

You can come back to this page in order to check that your current time zone settings are accurate.
